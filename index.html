GST

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based indirect tax introduced in India on 1st July 2017. It replaced multiple cascading taxes levied by the central and state governments, such as:

  • Central Taxes: Excise Duty, Service Tax, VAT, CST, etc.
  • State Taxes: VAT, Entertainment Tax, Luxury Tax, Octroi, etc.

GST is a single tax system applied at every stage of the supply chain, ensuring transparency and reducing tax evasion.GST was introduced to simplify Indiaâ€™s tax system, reduce corruption, and create a unified national market. Despite initial challenges, it has improved tax compliance and boosted economic growth.
